Structural Health Monitoring

Building and urban infrastructure could be monitor and track with natural vibration frequency. This method reducing the cost of maintenance and risk of a collision due to detecting damage in the early stage. MEMS accelerometer and IoT sensors could realize the idea of SHM using vibration monitoring. State-of-the-art low-cost SHM sensors lead to use in the future increasingly.

Applications

  • Urban infrastructure.
  • Antiquities preservation.
  • Building.
